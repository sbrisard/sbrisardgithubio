MathJax now accepts equation numbers

As of version 2.0, MathJax can number equations automatically. To enable this feature, the following lines must be added before the <script> tag that loads MathJax itself 

<script type="text/x-mathjax-config">
  MathJax.Hub.Config({
      TeX: {equationNumbers: {autoNumber: "AMS"}}
  });
</script>

Then, the following lines 

\begin{equation}
  \label{eq:1}
  (a + b)^2=a^2+2ab+b^2
\end{equation}

<p>
Eq. \eqref{eq:1} shows that the square of the sum of $a$ and $b$ is
<em>not</em> the sum of squares of $a$ and $b$.
<p>

produce

\begin{equation} \label{eq:1} (a + b)^2=a^2+2ab+b^2 \end{equation}

Eq. \eqref{eq:1} shows that the square of the sum of \(a\) and \(b\) is not the sum of squares of \(a\) and \(b\).

I will try and update this whole blog accordingly.
