Going public (take two)

Well, I've been silent for a looooong time. I'd like to revive this blog. Not sure I will ever finish this (excellent!) series on polarization techniques, though.

I have recently started to work on high performance computing for image analysis and numerical simulation of heterogeneous materials. I used to develop all my computations in Java, but recently moved to Python (why I did that may be the topic of a future post). I also started working with PETSc (using Lisandro Dalcin's excellent petsc4py Python bindings) as well as Cython. I intend to report on technicalities regarding these tools/libraries.

Enjoy your read!
